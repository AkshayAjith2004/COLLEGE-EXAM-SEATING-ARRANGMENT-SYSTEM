Our Institution

College of Engineering Poonjar was established in 2000 By Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), an autonomous educational institution under the Government of Kerala. The management of IHRD is vested with a Governing Body composed of with the Hon'ble Minister of Education, Kerala State, as the Chairman and Additional Chief Secretary, Higher Education Department, Government of Kerala as the Vice-Chairman. IHRD is based on the principle, "Think Globally and Act Locally" and its associate institutions are located in diverse locations in Kerala to achieve this goal. College of Engineering Poonjar is located in a scenic place on a calm hilly region with pure air and greenery congenial for any student to pursue learning with dedication.

Vision

Scientific advancement, Technological progress and Economic growth of the Country through human resources development adhering to the principle "Think Globally, and Act Locally".

Mission

About the System

The traditional method of arranging seats manually in educational institutions is time-consuming, prone to seat conflicts, and highly tedious. This portal solves those limitations by offering role-based automation:

  • Intelligent Seating Generator: Uses round-robin interleaving to place students from different semesters/branches next to each other, preventing collusion.
  • Print-Ready Documents: Download door plans, desk slips, notice board lists, and branch summaries in one click.
  • Subject & Hall Allocator: Manage room dimensions (rows and columns) and associate subjects and teachers instantly.
Secure Access
Separate logins for Students, Teachers, Controllers, and Admins with Django Auth protection.
Interleaved Seating
Weave BCA, MCA, and B.Tech students in rows and columns to maintain examination integrity.
Desk Slips & Reports
Generate and print classroom desk labels, signature sheets, and room door layouts.
Manual Adjustments
Allows controllers to manually swap students or clear seats after automatic allocation.
